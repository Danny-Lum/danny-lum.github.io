Seize The Day

Seize The Day

Typography / Print Communication | Duration: 1 month

For this project, I decided to use the phrase “Seize The Day”. The three fonts that I focused on were Onyx, Broadway, and Old English. Each font that I chose were unique in their way. Onyx is a condensed and stretched font with very sharp serifs, Broadway had thick strokes on vertical lines and thin strokes on horizontal lines, and lastly, Old English has interesting curved line details inside the capital letters as well as it was oddly sharp with its lower case characters.

For my design, I went with Broadway as the base and then made the top half more Onyx focus where I took the characteristics of the sharp serifs and the bottom half focused more towards the Old English. The overall shape
was sharp like Old English lowercase letters as well as I took the idea of implementing the curved designs from the capital letter.
